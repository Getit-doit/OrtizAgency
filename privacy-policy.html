Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Ortiz Agency respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded when you visit this website or contact us regarding insurance products and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and other contact details. If you request a quote or insurance information, we may collect additional details necessary to evaluate coverage options and respond to your request.

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, such as browser type, device information, and pages visited, to help improve website functionality and performance.

How We Use Information

Information collected is used to respond to inquiries, provide insurance quotes, service policies, communicate with you regarding your request, and maintain our business relationship. Information may also be used for internal business purposes, including improving our services and website experience.

Information Sharing

Ortiz Agency does not sell or rent your personal information. Information may be shared with insurance carriers, service providers, or other third parties only as necessary to provide requested insurance services, comply with legal obligations, or protect against fraud or unauthorized activity.

TCPA & SMS/Text Messaging Consent

By providing your telephone number to Ortiz Agency, you expressly consent to be contacted by Ortiz Agency, its representatives, or affiliated service providers by telephone call or text message, including through the use of automated dialing systems or prerecorded messages, for purposes related to insurance quotes, coverage information, policy servicing, and customer support, in accordance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

Consent to receive calls or text messages is not a condition of purchasing any insurance product. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier. You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying “STOP” to any text message or by contacting Ortiz Agency directly.

Texas-Specific Privacy Notice

For residents of the State of Texas, Ortiz Agency complies with applicable Texas privacy laws and insurance regulations governing the collection, use, and sharing of personal information. Nonpublic personal information is collected and disclosed only as permitted by law and for purposes directly related to insurance transactions, underwriting, servicing, or claims handling.

Texas residents may request information regarding the personal data we maintain, request corrections to inaccurate information, or inquire about our information practices by contacting Ortiz Agency using the information provided below.

Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children under the age of 13. Ortiz Agency does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, TCPA consent, or our information practices, please contact Ortiz Agency directly.
