Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect phone numbers from users when they voluntarily provide them for the purpose of receiving communication from us. Phone numbers are collected directly from consumers through web forms, applications, and inquiries.

2. Consent to Contact

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive communications from us via SMS, phone calls, or other methods. You acknowledge and agree that:

  • Your phone number may be used to contact you regarding services and offers
  • Messages may be sent using an automatic dialing system and/or prerecorded or artificial voice
  • Providing your phone number constitutes consent to contact you
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase or service with Ortiz Agency

3. Types of Communications

We may send you the following types of communications:

  • Service updates and informational messages
  • Promotional offers and marketing materials
  • Account, service, or policy-related notifications
  • Surveys and feedback requests
  • Customer service and support messages

4. Message Frequency

The frequency of communications may vary based on your interaction with us and the information you provided. We do not limit the frequency of messages, though we strive to keep communications reasonable and relevant.

5. Fees and Charges

Please be aware that message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile service plan. You are responsible for any charges incurred from your mobile service provider. We recommend checking with your carrier for details about your plan's messaging and data rates.

6. Opt-Out / Revocation of Consent

You can opt-out of receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any text message from us
  • Contacting us directly at [insert contact email/phone]
  • Using the unsubscribe link in any email communication

Upon receipt of your opt-out request, we will process your request promptly and honor it immediately. You will no longer receive messages from us unless you choose to re-engage.

7. Use of Phone Numbers

Your phone number is used exclusively for:

  • Delivering communications and messages you consent to receive
  • Providing customer service and support
  • Managing your account and service interactions
  • Improving our services and customer experience

8. Sharing of Phone Numbers

Regarding your phone number and personal information:

  • We may share your phone number with trusted third-party service providers (such as SMS platforms) solely for the purpose of delivering communications on our behalf
  • Any third-party service providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify
  • We do not sell or share your phone number for unrelated marketing purposes
  • We do not rent or lease your phone number to third parties

9. Consent Recordkeeping

Ortiz Agency maintains detailed records of:

  • All consumer consents to receive communications
  • Opt-out requests and the dates they were received
  • Communication preferences and any updates made

These records are maintained to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

10. Changes to Communication Preferences

You may update or revoke your communication preferences at any time by:

  • Contacting us directly at [insert contact email]
  • Replying "STOP" to any SMS message
  • Updating your preferences through your account portal

Changes to your preferences will be processed immediately.

11. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your phone number and personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to amend this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy to this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed of how we protect your information.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our communication practices, please contact us at:
